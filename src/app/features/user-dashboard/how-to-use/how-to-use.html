Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these simple steps to get started

1

Create an Account

Sign up with your email and create a secure password.

2

Deposit Funds

Add funds to your wallet using PayPal, Payoneer, or Credit Card.

3

Select Platform

Choose the social media platform you want to grow on.

4

Choose Services

Pick the services you need (followers, likes, views, etc.).

5

Add to Cart

Select quantities and add multiple services to your cart.

6

Place Order

Review your cart and place a mass order for all services.

7

Track Progress

Monitor your order status in the Order History page.

Pro Tips

  • Start with smaller orders to test the quality
  • Use the cart for bulk orders to save time
  • Keep your wallet funded for quick ordering
  • Check order history to track delivery progress
  • Refer friends to earn bonus credits
